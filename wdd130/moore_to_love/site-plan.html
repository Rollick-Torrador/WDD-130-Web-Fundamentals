Home page

WHAT WE DO

Moore to Love is a place for people to find a way to connect more deeply with their partner. Whether you have been married for sixty years or dating for a few months, everyone needs the opportunity to work together on themselves and their relationships. Learn about your options, get to know our therapists, and book a life-changing appointment today.

>

OUR THERAPISTS

Moore to Love has three main therapists who specialise in different areas of treatment. Each one has years of experience helping couples just like you. First, we have Audrey Moore, our founder. She is EMDR certified and works primarily with married couples. Our second therapist is Shelly Morrison. Working primarily with younger families, she uses ART therapy to help repair relationships that need a special touch. Our final therapist is Jason Conchake. With thirty years in the industry and a specialization in CBT therapy, he is always a great option for those who want to stretch out of their comfort zones to reach new heights. No matter your choice, our therapists know exactly how to guide you to become your best self!

Contact Us

HOW CAN YOU CONTACT US?

>

Just fill out the box below with your name, number, and your preferred meeting times, and we’ll reach out to schedule your first appointment. Scheduling is completely hassle-free. Once your appointment is set, make sure to arrive 15 minutes early before your first appointment to fill out the appropriate paperwork, and your therapist will be right with you. We look forward to joining you on your self-improvement journey.

>

DO I NEED ANY SPECIAL INFORMATION?

>

To schedule an appointment with one of our therapists, all you need is your contact information, including the names of you and your partner, which therapist you’d like to meet with, and a preferred meeting time. Then, you can use our “contact us” page to get in touch with our office to schedule that first meeting with your therapist.

Our Services

HOW CAN WE HELP YOU?

The questions we can help you answer include: how can I improve my marriage, where do I go for help, and is there a path forward? The answer is here at Moore to Love, where our specially trained Clinical Therapists help couples work through problems and get back to a happy equilibrium. No one wants their relationships to fail, but sometimes a little work is needed. We can make that work a little easier.

WHAT IS THERE TO DO?

The following are activities that you can complete on our site. A connection quiz, where you learn about your partner’s love language and how to help them better. Reading about our therapists and finding the best fit for you and your partner. Learning about your options for therapy and the different types of services our therapists perform. Finally, once you are ready, head on over to our “contact us” page to schedule your first appointment.

WHY SCHEDULE WITH MOORE TO LOVE?

You’ll likely come to our site from personal references and online recommendations to find a place to resolve issues that might be holding your relationship back. Our purpose is to find a way to get you to schedule your first appointment on our “contact us” page and get that first appointment scheduled. Now, why choose Moore to Love over another group of therapists? At Moore to Love, we understand that everyone has troubles, and our therapists are the best in the industry at helping couples resolve differences, improve themselves, and in turn, improve their relationships. You choose us because you are serious about finding and making meaningful change!
