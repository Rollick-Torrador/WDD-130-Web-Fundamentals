What We Do

Moore to Love is a place for people to find a way to connect more deeply with their partner. Whether you have been married for sixty years or dating for a few months, everyone needs the opportunity to work together on themselves and their relationships. Learn about your options, get to know our therapists, and book a life-changing appointment today.

Our Therapists

Moore to Love has three main therapists who specialize in different areas of treatment.

Audrey Moore is our founder. She is EMDR certified and works primarily with married couples.

Shelly works primarily with younger families and uses ART therapy to help repair relationships that need a special touch.

Jason has over thirty years in the industry and specializes in CBT therapy. He helps people stretch out of their comfort zones to reach new heights.
