What We Do

Moore to Love is a place for people to find a way to connect more deeply with their partner. Whether you have been married for sixty years or dating for a few months, everyone needs the opportunity to work together on themselves and their relationships. Learn about your options, get to know our therapists, and book a life-changing appointment today.

Our Therapists

Moore to Love has three main therapists who specialise in different areas of treatment. Each one has years of experience helping couples just like you. No matter your choice, our therapists know exactly how to guide you to become your best self!

Audrey Moore

Audrey Moore is our founder. She is EMDR certified and works primarily with married couples.

Shelly Morrison

Shelly Morrison works primarily with younger families, she uses ART therapy to help repair relationships that need a special touch.

Jason Conchake

Jason Conchake has over thirty years in the industry and a specialization in CBT therapy, he is always a great option for those who want to stretch out of their comfort zones to reach new heights.
